Introduction

In today's digital landscape, content is king. However, creating fresh content can be time-consuming and resource-intensive for small businesses. This guide presents an effective AI content repurposing workflow designed to streamline content creation using AI tools. Readers will learn how to maximize the value of existing content, leverage AI for enhanced productivity, and implement strategies that lead to better engagement.

What you need to know first

Before diving into AI content repurposing, it's important to grasp some foundational concepts:

  • Understanding content formats: Blog posts, videos, podcasts, social media posts, etc.
  • Familiarity with AI tools capable of content generation and transformation, such as Descript and Pictory.
  • Basic knowledge of your audience and target platforms for effective distribution.

Decision rules:

When to use this approach:

  • When you have existing content that can be transformed into new formats.
  • If you're looking to keep your audience engaged with minimal effort.
  • When resources for producing fresh content are limited.

Tradeoffs:

Pros/Cons:

Understanding the benefits and drawbacks is crucial:

  • Pros: Saves time, enhances marketing efficiency, boosts content lifespan.
  • Cons: Potential loss of original content's nuance, dependency on AI for quality.

Failure modes:

What can go wrong and how to avoid it:

  • Over-reliance on AI can lead to generic content; balance AI use with human creativity.
  • Not all formats translate well; assess compatibility before repurposing.
  • Ignoring audience preferences may result in reduced engagement; use analytics to guide decisions.

SOP checklist:

Step-by-step checklist:

  1. Identify high-performing content pieces.
  2. Choose appropriate AI tools for repurposing.
  3. Transform the content into different formats.
  4. Optimize the repurposed content for each platform.
  5. Schedule and publish the new content.
  6. Analyze performance and collect feedback.

Template 1:

Content Repurposing Template:

Original Blog Post Title: "5 Marketing Strategies for 2023"
Key Points:
- Utilize social media advertising
- Leverage customer testimonials

Podcast Episode Template:

Draft an engaging introduction, ask insightful questions for each key topic, and include a recap of the main points at the end.

Step-by-step workflow

  1. Review analytics to identify top-performing content.
  2. Select an AI tool for repurposing, such as Descript for videos.
  3. Use the template to create a detailed plan for transforming the content.
  4. Transform and optimize the content using the AI tool.
  5. Publish the content across chosen platforms.
  6. Monitor engagement and collect performance metrics.

Try it yourself: Build your own AI prompt

Follow this workflow: Human → Prompt #1 (to a general AI) → Prompt #2 (for Descript) → Use in the tool. Use the following meta-prompt:

Role: You are a content strategist with expertise in repurposing content for diverse formats.
Goal: The user wants to maximize the reach of a blog post by converting it into a video.
Task: Please create a prompt that will guide the AI to generate a video script from a provided blog post.
Uncertainty Flagging: If you are unsure about any element of the blog post, state so and ask for clarification before proceeding.
Permission to ask clarifying questions: If the context is insufficient, you may (and should) ask the user for more details.

When NOT to use this

Avoid using AI content repurposing when:

  • Up-to-the-minute news requires immediate content creation that at times may not benefit from repurposing.
  • Audience feedback indicates a desire for fresh, original content instead of reformatted material.

FAQ

  • How can I determine which content to repurpose? Review performance analytics to find high-engagement content.
  • Are there risks involved with AI content generation? Yes, the primary risk is generic content; ensure to add your unique voice.
  • Can I use AI tools for social media posts? Absolutely! Tools like ChatGPT can help create eye-catching social media content.
