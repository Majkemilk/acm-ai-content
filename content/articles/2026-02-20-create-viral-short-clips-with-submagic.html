Introduction

In today’s fast-paced digital world, short-form video content has become essential for engaging viewers quickly and effectively. This article will guide you on how to create viral short clips from long videos using Submagic, an AI-powered tool designed to simplify video editing. By the end, you will understand the prerequisites, decision rules, potential trade-offs, and steps to effectively use Submagic for your content strategy.

What you need to know first

Before diving into the creation process, it's important to understand a few key concepts:

  • Short-form video content: Typically lasts under a minute, focusing on quick, digestible information suitable for platforms like TikTok or Instagram.
  • Long-form video content: Videos that can range from several minutes to hours, often containing in-depth discussions or storytelling.
  • AI-powered editing: Utilizing artificial intelligence to automate time-consuming tasks such as cutting, trimming, and highlighting key moments in video footage.

Decision rules:

Decision rules:

  • If you have a long video that contains engaging content, consider using Submagic to create short clips.
  • Use this approach when targeting audiences who prefer quick consumption of information.
  • Opt for AI editing when time and efficiency are of the essence, particularly for marketing strategies.

Tradeoffs:

Tradeoffs:

  • Pros: Faster editing process, ability to create multiple clips from a single video, enhanced engagement potential.
  • Cons: Less control over the final product compared to manual editing, reliance on AI algorithms which may misinterpret context.

Failure modes:

Failure modes:

  • AI may cut out essential context, leading to confusing clips. Always review edited clips before publishing.
  • If the original video is of low quality, the output clips may not perform well. Ensure source videos are high-quality.
  • Not aligning the clips with target audience preferences could reduce engagement. Always understand your audience's needs.

SOP checklist:

SOP checklist:

  • Gather all long-form video content you intend to edit.
  • Log into your Submagic account.
  • Upload the long-form video file to the platform.
  • Select themes or styles for the short clips.
  • Initiate the AI editing process to generate short clips.
  • Review each clip for quality and coherence.
  • Download and distribute on your preferred video platforms.

Step-by-step workflow

The following steps outline the main process for creating short clips:

  1. Human → Prompt #1 (to a general AI) → AI returns Prompt #2 (ready-to-paste prompt for the specific tool) → Use Prompt #2 in the tool.
  2. Gather your long video files together.
  3. Log into your Submagic account and upload the videos.

### Prompt for Identifying Strong Segments for Short Clips

**Objective:** Extract engaging 30-second clips from a long educational video on time management that emphasize practical techniques.

#### Key Considerations:
1. **Target Audience**: Who is the primary audience for the clips? (e.g., students, professionals, busy parents)
2. **Tone & Style**: What tone should the clips convey? (e.g., motivational, instructional, casual)
3. **Focus Techniques**: What specific time management techniques should we highlight? (e.g., prioritization, the Pomodoro Technique, scheduling tips)
4. **Visual Style**: Do you have any preferred visual styles for the clips? (e.g., animated text, dynamic transitions, direct-to-camera speaking)
5. **Call to Action**: Are there specific calls to action or encouraging messages to include in the clips?

#### Steps to Identify Segments:
1. **Review Video Content**: Watch the entire video and take notes on time stamps where practical techniques are introduced.
2. **Identify Key Points**: Look for segments that:
   - Clearly explain a time management technique.
   - Include relatable scenarios or examples that the audience can connect with.
   - Feature energetic or passionate delivery that draws viewers in.
3. **Assess Visual Appeal**: Check for segments with:
   - Engaging visuals, graphics, or demonstrations.
   - Clear and concise explanations that can be easily understood in 30 seconds.
4. **Gather Quotes**: Identify any strong quotes or phrases that encapsulate the essence of the technique.

#### Example of Desired Segments:
- **Technique Explanation**: A 30-second clip where the presenter explains the Pomodoro Technique with a visual stopwatch.
- **Relatable Scenario**: A segment showcasing a common time management mistake, leading to a practical tip for overcoming it.
- **Motivational Call to Action**: A powerful closing statement that encourages viewers to implement what they've learned.

### Feedback Request:
Please provide clarity on the following:
- What is the primary audience for these clips?
- What editing styles do you prefer or envision for the final output?
- Are there specific techniques or key messages you want to prioritize?

When NOT to use this

Avoid using Submagic to create viral short clips if:

  • The original long-form video lacks engaging content.
  • You're aiming for highly specific editing that requires nuanced human intervention.
  • You don’t have a clear understanding of your target audience's preferences.

FAQ

  • How long does it take to create clips with Submagic? The AI can quickly generate clips based on the length and complexity of the video, typically within minutes.
  • Can I manually adjust the AI-generated clips? Yes, you can review and make changes to ensure the clips meet your quality standards.
  • Is Submagic suitable for all types of content? While it excels in various formats, it’s best suited for content that naturally lends itself to short, engaging segments.
