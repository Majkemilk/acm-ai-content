Introduction

In the age of social media, short-form content has taken the spotlight. This article will guide you on how to create viral short clips from long videos using Submagic, an AI-powered video editing tool that simplifies the process of transforming lengthy footage into engaging snippets. By the end, you’ll be equipped to create compelling clips that resonate with your audience and drive engagement.

What you need to know first

Before diving into clip creation, it’s essential to understand the following key concepts:

  • Content Selection: Not all parts of a video will be suitable for short clips. Identifying key moments is crucial.
  • Audience Engagement: Understanding what captivates your audience will help in crafting more effective clips.
  • Editing Basics: Familiarity with basic video editing concepts can enhance your output.

Decision rules:

When to use this approach:

  • When you have long video content that is packed with valuable information.
  • If you aim to boost your social media engagement by leveraging trending content formats.
  • When your objective is to repurpose existing video content efficiently.

Tradeoffs:

Pros & Cons:

  • Pros:
    • Quick and easy process using Submagic.
    • Enhances viewer retention and engagement.
    • Allows for a creative way to present key insights.
  • Cons:
    • Requires a clear understanding of your audience’s preferences.
    • Risk of losing essential context if not edited properly.
    • Dependence on AI may reduce personalization in the editing process.

Failure modes:

What can go wrong:

  • Critical points might be missed if AI analysis is insufficient.
  • Clips may not resonate with your audience if the wrong segments are chosen.
  • Over-reliance on AI can lead to generic content rather than unique clips.

To avoid these risks, always review and refine the AI-generated clips before publishing.

SOP checklist:

Step-by-step checklist:

  1. Upload your long video content to Submagic.
  2. Select the key moments based on your content selection criteria.
  3. Use AI tools for automated editing to create short clips.
  4. Review the generated clips for context and audience engagement.
  5. Finalize and export the clips in a suitable format for social media.
  6. Analyze performance metrics to assess engagement and effectiveness of the clips.

Step-by-step workflow

  1. Analyze your long video content to identify potential highlights.
  2. Utilize Submagic to automate the selection of key segments.
  3. Edit and refine selected clips to ensure they are engaging and contextually appropriate.
  4. Export the clips for distribution across appropriate platforms.
  5. Monitor audience reactions and adapt future content based on feedback.

When NOT to use this

Avoid using AI for creating short clips in the following scenarios: when your content requires nuanced understanding, when the source material is poorly structured, or if your target audience prefers more in-depth analysis over brief highlights.

FAQ

  • What types of videos work best for this approach? Generally, tutorial, webinar, or educational videos are ideal, as they often contain valuable information that can be extracted into short clips.
  • How long should the short clips be? The ideal length is usually between 30 seconds to 1 minute to maintain viewer engagement.
  • Can I use Submagic for clips other than social media? Yes, Submagic can be used for any platform requiring short video snippets, including presentations and marketing materials.
