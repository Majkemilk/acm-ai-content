What to look for when choosing bicycle identification systems

Choosing the right bicycle identification system involves a few key factors. First, make sure the system is easy to use and set up. Second, consider whether it offers recovery services. Lastly, check for features like visibility and durability. Here are some specifics to keep in mind:

  • Ease of Use: Look for systems that require minimal effort to register and maintain.
  • Recovery Support: Some systems provide assistance in recovering stolen bikes.
  • Durability: The identification system should withstand weather elements.
  • Visibility: Ensure that the ID is easily visible or can be scanned quickly.
